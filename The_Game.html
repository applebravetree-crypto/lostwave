Search

Under The White Bible Law

On 28 June 2026, while searching for videos of "Walk Away" on YouTube, I found a video of Under The White Bible Law, the only known-so-far album by The Game. It was uploaded by a channel named Under The Waves, a "channel that is dedicated to uploading obscure 80s music that is either underrated, hard to find or unavailable on streaming services or YouTube". This was the first evidence of songs by The Game other than "Walk Away" & "Under The White Bible Law" (The latter I had discovered shortly after discovering "Walk Away").

On 1 July 2026, I found a page on Discogs listing the songs in this album, strongly confirming that it did in-fact exist. The aforementioned video on YouTube also lists the songs in its description—though without "Over The Sea"— but this page once again strongly confirms their veracity.
